Careers

QGS Technologies is developing solutions at the intersection of key technologies today: Artificial Intelligence, Mixed Reality, and Blockchain. Occasionally we’ll post here as interesting internship and apprenticeship opportunities arise. At the moment, we are keen to hear from any prospective applicant who has: (1) a passion for international politics and its impact on global insurance and capital markets. (2) a habit of learning and a dedication to tackle seemingly intractable questions in software design, international affairs, and capital markets. (3) proven dedication to remain ahead of the curve as far as world megatrends are involved. More specifically:

Computer Vision Engineer (C/C++, Clojure/Lisp, NodeJS)

Junior Blockchain Engineer (C++ or Java, Javascript, and  willingness to train on the job)

Dev Ops Engineer (Ansible, AWS/Google Cloud/IBM BlueMix, Agile+Scrum, Database experience with EB2, Sybase/SQL)

Front-end Lead (React, WebVR/ReactVR, Socket.io, NodeJS)

If any of these positions suit your past, or future desired experience, please get in touch with us here. When emailing, please include CV, sample code or past project links or Github link on past contributions. We’re also quite open to chat if you’d like to explore whether there could be a good fit.
